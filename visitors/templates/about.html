Meet Yoram David – Osteopath and Personal Trainer

Yoram David – Osteopath graduated from Wingate School of Osteopathy in 2018. Since then he has been working at Jerusalem helping all his patients feel better, move better and live active lives.

Yoram uses a wide variety of hands-on treatment techniques including; massage, stretching, gentle joint mobilisation and manipulation, dry needling, MET, ART and lots of rehabilitation and exercise prescriptions. With this special blend of therapies combined with a holistic approach to patient care, Yoram is able to get great results and helps his patients feel better faster.
